01 — Snapshot

Project Overview

The Laura Sanchez identity places the artist at the center — a mark that functions as both signature and symbol, rooted in nature and personal expression.

Overall Concept

The identity integrates a tulip symbol directly into the artist’s name. Paired with expressive typography, the system bridges personal artistry and the organic qualities of growth, renewal, and beauty.

Artist Representation

Using “Laura Sanchez” as the primary mark keeps the artist at the heart of the brand. Her name becomes more than a signature — it represents her vision and ensures every piece reflects an authentic voice.

Key Components

  • Wordmark: Alverata Informal
  • Logomark: Tulip “U” symbol
  • Secondary type: Lato for clarity and versatility
  • Palette: Soft black + warm highlight + airy accents

02 — Logo System

Wordmark & Logomark

The primary logo is built to work first in one color for maximum versatility. Full-color executions are used as alternate moments when production and background conditions support it.

Primary One-Color Wordmark

The wordmark uses Alverata Informal for an expressive, handcrafted voice. The one-color execution is the default for reliable reproduction across print, stamps, embroidery, and small-scale use — keeping the mark consistent everywhere.

Brand role: Primary signature across digital and print.

Tulip “U” Logomark

The “U” is stylized into a tulip — a symbol of beauty, growth, and renewal — designed to work as a standalone mark across icons, stamps, and repeatable brand patterns.

Full-Color Wordmark (Alternative)

A color-forward version for feature moments — ideal for digital highlights, editorial layouts, or controlled backgrounds where the palette can shine.

Stacked Lockup

A compact option for square and vertical spaces — useful for avatars, stamps, and smaller placements where the primary wordmark won’t fit cleanly.

Logo Usage Notes

  • Primary: Default to the one-color wordmark for consistent reproduction.
  • Secondary: Use the tulip “U” as an icon, stamp, pattern, or small-space brand cue.
  • Alternative: Use the full-color wordmark when backgrounds + production support it.
  • Do not: distort, skew, outline, or add heavy effects to the wordmark or tulip symbol.

03 — Typography

Typeface System

Laura Sanchez pairs the expressive elegance of Alverata Informal with the clean versatility of Lato. This balances character and readability — Alverata brings the artistic voice, and Lato ensures clarity across digital and print applications.

Primary Typeface — Alverata Informal

Expressive, humanist curves and a handcrafted warmth make Alverata Informal ideal for the wordmark and key brand moments.

Secondary Typeface — Lato

Lato supports body copy and informational text with modern simplicity and excellent legibility, keeping the system clear and functional.

Typography Roles

  • Alverata Informal: Wordmark, headlines, signature moments
  • Lato: Body copy, captions, supporting text, digital UI
  • Balance: Character + clarity across all brand touchpoints

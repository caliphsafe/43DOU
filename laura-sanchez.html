01 — Snapshot

Project Overview

The Laura Sanchez identity places the artist at the center — a mark that functions as both signature and symbol, rooted in nature and personal expression.

Overall Concept

The identity integrates a tulip symbol directly into the artist’s name. Paired with expressive typography, the system bridges personal artistry and the organic qualities of growth, renewal, and beauty.

Artist Representation

Using “Laura Sanchez” as the primary mark keeps the artist at the heart of the brand. Her name becomes more than a signature — it represents her vision and ensures every piece reflects an authentic voice.

Key Components

  • Wordmark: Alverata Informal
  • Logomark: Tulip “U” symbol
  • Secondary type: Lato for clarity and versatility
  • Palette: Soft black + warm highlight + airy accents

02 — Logo System

Wordmark & Logomark

The wordmark leads with personality and artistry. The logomark turns the “U” into a tulip — creating a recognizable symbol that can stand alone.

Wordmark

The wordmark uses Alverata Informal, a typeface known for its blend of historical roots and contemporary informality. Its soft, humanist curves convey individuality and approachability, aligning with the organic nature of Laura’s work.

Brand role: Primary signature across digital and print.

Tulip “U” Logomark

The “U” is stylized into a tulip flower — a symbol of beauty, growth, and renewal — designed to work as a standalone brand mark.

Stacked Lockup

A compact option for square and vertical spaces — useful for avatars, stamps, and smaller placements.

One-Color Version

A production-safe version built for versatile usage across print, stamps, and limited-color applications.

Logo Usage Notes

  • Clear space: Maintain comfortable breathing room around the mark for a premium feel.
  • Do not: distort, skew, outline, or add heavy effects to the wordmark or tulip symbol.
  • Consistency: Use the stacked lockup only where the primary wordmark won’t fit cleanly.

03 — Typography

Typeface System

Laura Sanchez pairs the expressive elegance of Alverata Informal with the clean versatility of Lato. This balances character and readability — Alverata brings the artistic voice, and Lato ensures clarity across digital and print applications.

Primary Typeface — Alverata Informal

Expressive, humanist curves and a handcrafted warmth make Alverata Informal ideal for the wordmark and key brand moments.

Secondary Typeface — Lato

Lato supports body copy and informational text with modern simplicity and excellent legibility, keeping the system clear and functional.

Typography Roles

  • Alverata Informal: Wordmark, headlines, signature moments
  • Lato: Body copy, captions, supporting text, digital UI
  • Balance: Character + clarity across all brand touchpoints

05 — Applications

Brand in the Real World

A few high-impact applications demonstrating how the wordmark and tulip logomark hold up across touchpoints.

Stationery

A refined document system where typography and spacing do the work.

Social Templates

Consistent tone across posts with clear type hierarchy and accent color usage.

Packaging / Labels

Tulip mark works as a recognizable stamp while the wordmark leads the system.

Application Guidance

  • Primary moments: Use the full wordmark for signature placements.
  • Small spaces: Use the tulip “U” mark as an icon or stamp.
  • Consistency: Keep Alverata for headlines and Lato for body text to maintain balance.

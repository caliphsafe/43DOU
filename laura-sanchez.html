Logo Rules (Simple + Professional)

  • Clear space: Keep at least 1× the height of the “L” around the mark.
  • Minimum size: Ensure readability at small sizes (test at 24px / 0.33in).
  • Do not: stretch, skew, add outlines, or add drop shadows to the logo.

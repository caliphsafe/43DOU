01 — Snapshot + Logo

Identity System

The Laura Sanchez identity places the artist at the center — a mark that functions as both signature and symbol, rooted in nature and personal expression.

Snapshot

  • Concept: Artist-first identity bridging personal expression, artistry, and nature
  • Wordmark: Alverata Informal (signature personality)
  • Logomark: Tulip “U” symbol (recognizable standalone mark)
  • System: One mark that scales across digital + print

Logo Notes

  • Primary: Wordmark for hero placements and signatures
  • Icon: Tulip “U” for small spaces and stamps
  • Do not: distort, outline, skew, or add heavy effects
